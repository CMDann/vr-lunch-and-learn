# Workshop Overview — PAINT THE VOID

**Presenter:** Daniel Blair, Founder & CTO — BSD XR / Instructor — Red River College Polytechnic

**Format:** 1 hour total — Part 1 (presentation, ~30 min) + Part 2 (hands-on VR session, 30–40 min)

**Audience:** Students, educators, and professionals encountering XR for the first time or looking to deepen their understanding of immersive technology.

---

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this workshop, participants will be able to:

1. Explain the XR spectrum from AR through VR and where different technologies sit on it
2. Describe the core design principles behind building safe, effective XR experiences
3. Identify the interaction modalities available in XR and when to use each
4. Understand how Three.js and WebXR are used to build browser-based immersive experiences
5. Name real career paths in XR and what skills they require
6. Articulate how generative AI is converging with XR to create adaptive experiences
7. Navigate a VR headset safely and create 3D art in OpenBrush

## Part 1 — Sections

| Section | Title | Slides | Key Ideas |
|---|---|---|---|
| 00 | Introduction | 0–2 | Who is BSD XR, who is Daniel Blair |
| 01 | VR & AR | 3–6 | XR spectrum, VR, AR, the science of presence |
| 02 | Designing an Experience | 7–15 | Design principles, interaction modalities, live Three.js build |
| 03 | Opportunity | 16–18 | What you can build, jobs in XR, Manitoba ecosystem |
| 04 | Generative AI in XR | 19–23 | AI + XR convergence, live AI demo, what comes next |

---

## Part 2 — Activities

| Activity | Duration | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Device Orientation | 0:00–0:05 | Headsets on and calibrated |
| 01 — Draw Your Name | 0:05–0:20 | First steps in 3D space with Ink/Marker/Light brushes |
| 02 — Simple Object | 0:20–0:35 | Build a recognizable 3D form with Geometry/Icing/Hypercolor |
| 03 — Collaborative Build | 0:35–0:45 | One shared scene, rotating headset (optional) |
| Reflect + Reset | 0:45–0:50 | Share, discuss, pack down |

---

## Hardware Required

- Meta Quest headset(s) with OpenBrush installed
- Guardian boundary configured in each headset
- Minimum 2m × 2m clear floor space per headset
