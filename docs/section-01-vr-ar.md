## Slide 6 — The Science of Presence

**WHY IT WORKS**

### THE SCIENCE OF PRESENCE

**01 — SPATIAL MEMORY**
Humans retain information 3–4× better when learned in 3D spatial context. The environment becomes part of the memory.

**02 — EMBODIMENT**
Presence triggers genuine physiological and psychological responses. The brain treats virtual events as real.

**03 — SAFE FAILURE**
High-risk procedures trained without consequence. Safety without exposure. Error as learning, not liability.
